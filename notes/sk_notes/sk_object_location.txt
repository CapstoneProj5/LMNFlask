***
    "Location is a simplified object representing a city on Songkick"
***


======= FORMAT =======

    city: (str)

    lat: (float) [nullable]

    lng: (float) [nullable]


======= EXAMPLE GIVEN ========

      {
        "city":"San Francisco, CA, US",
        "lat":37.7842398,
        "lng":-122.4332937
      }
